FAQs

Why DesignDev and why wouldn't I just hire a full-time designer?
Great question! Opting for a full-time senior-level designer can be costly, with an annual expense exceeding $147,683 , not to mention benefits. Moreover, you might not always have a consistent flow of work, leading to paying for unutilized time. With our monthly plan, you have the flexibility to pause and resume your subscription as needed, ensuring you only pay when you have tasks for your designer.
Is there a limit to the number of design requests I can make?
No limits! Once subscribed, you can add as many design requests to your queue as you'd like, ensuring your creative needs are met efficiently and seamlessly.
How quickly will I receive my designs?
Expect swift turnaround times! Most requests are completed within two days or less, although more complex projects may take a bit longer.
Who are the masterminds behind Designdev?
Surprise! Designdev is an agency led by a dynamic duo - the Founder, a skilled designer, and the Developer, who personally oversees your projects. Together, they bring a harmonious blend of creativity and technical expertise to elevate your brand.
Tell me about the pause feature. How does it work?
We get it, not every month is jam-packed with design needs. Maybe you just have one or two projects right now. That's where the pause feature comes to the rescue. Our billing cycles run on a 31-day period. So, let's say you subscribe to our plan and use our service for 5 days, and then you decide to take a break. When you pause your subscription, the billing cycle stops too. You'll have 26 days of service left, ready to be used whenever you have more design tasks in the future. It's a flexible way to make sure you only pay for what you need.
What design programs and programming language do you use?
Most requests are designed using Figma ensuring compatibility and seamless collaboration. As for development, we employ React.js to bring your digital projects to life.
How can I request designs?
Flexibility is our forte! You can request designs directly through Trello, share Google docs or wireframes, or even record a brief Loom video for a personalized touch. If it's linkable or shareable in Trello, we've got you covered.
What if I'm not thrilled with the design?
No stress! We're committed to your satisfaction. We'll revise the design until it aligns perfectly with your vision.
What if I only have a single request?
No problem at all! Pause your subscription when your needs are met, and resume whenever you're ready for more outstanding designs.
Are there any design & development areas you don't cover?
Indeed. Designdev doesn't handle 3D modeling, animated graphics, mobile app development , complex packaging, or extensive print design (magazines, books, etc.).
Is there a refund policy?
Due to the exceptional quality of our work, we don't issue refunds. We're confident you'll love what we create for you!
Can I request rush orders or expedited delivery?
Absolutely! We understand that some projects have tight deadlines. Contact us directly, and we'll do our best to accommodate your urgent design needs.
What level of customization is available for my designs?
We pride ourselves on tailoring designs to your unique brand identity. From color schemes to typography, we ensure your brand personality shines through in every project.
In what sector do you specialize?
We specialize in all sectors and industries! DesignDev is equipped to handle design projects across various fields, ensuring a customized and impactful approach tailored to your specific industry needs.
How secure is my confidential information and project data?
Rest assured, we take data security seriously. Your information is handled with the utmost confidentiality, and our systems are equipped with industry-standard security measures.
How does collaboration work with Designdev?
Collaboration is seamless with our user-friendly platform. You can provide feedback, track progress, and communicate directly with the Founder and Developer through your preferred channels, ensuring a smooth and transparent design process.
